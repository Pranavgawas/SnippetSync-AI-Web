Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2024

Overview

SnippetSyncAi is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how we handle your data.

Data We Collect

Free Plan: We collect nothing. All your snippets are stored locally on your device using Chrome's built-in storage. We never see, access, or transmit your data.

Pro Plan: If you choose to enable Cloud Sync, your snippets are encrypted and stored on secure servers (Firebase). We use this data only to sync your snippets across devices.

Data We Do NOT Collect

  • We do not track your browsing history
  • We do not sell your data to third parties
  • We do not use your data for advertising
  • We do not read the contents of your snippets

Payments

Payments are processed by Lemon Squeezy. We never see or store your credit card information.

Your Rights

You can delete all your data at any time by uninstalling the extension. For Pro users, contact us to request complete data deletion from our servers.
