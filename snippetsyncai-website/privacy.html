Privacy Policy for SnippetSyncAi

Last Updated: 23-12-25

1. Introduction

SnippetSyncAi ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how our Chrome Extension collects, uses, and discloses your information.

Our core philosophy is Offline-First. We believe your text data belongs to you.

2. Data We Collect

A. User Content (Snippets)

We do not collect, store, or transmit your text snippets to our servers.

All text snippets, shortcuts, and templates you create are stored locally on your device using the Chrome Storage API (`chrome.storage.local`). This data never leaves your browser unless you explicitly opt-in to future cloud sync features.

B. License Keys (Pro Plan)

If you upgrade to the Pro plan, you will be asked to enter a License Key.

  • What we do: We transmit this License Key to our payment provider (Lemon Squeezy) via a secure API request to verify your subscription status.
  • What we do NOT do: We do not send your snippets, browsing history, or personal information during this verification process. We only check if the key is valid.

C. Website Interaction

To function as a text expander, SnippetSyncAi requires access to the text fields on the websites you visit.

  • The extension detects when you type a specific keyword (e.g., `/email`).
  • It replaces that keyword with your saved snippet.
  • We do not log your keystrokes. The extension only "listens" locally for your specific shortcuts.

3. Third-Party Services

We use the following trusted third-party service to handle payments and license verification:

  • Lemon Squeezy: We use Lemon Squeezy to process payments and validate license keys. You can review their privacy policy at lemonsqueezy.com/privacy.

4. Permissions

SnippetSyncAi requests the following permissions to function:

  • Storage: To save your snippets locally on your device.
  • Host Permissions (<all_urls>): To allow the text expansion feature to work on any website you visit (e.g., Gmail, LinkedIn, Outlook).

5. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures. Your Pro status is verified using secure HTTPS requests. Since your personal text snippets are stored locally on your machine, they are as secure as your personal computer.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make significant changes (such as introducing Cloud Sync), we will notify you through the extension or our website.
