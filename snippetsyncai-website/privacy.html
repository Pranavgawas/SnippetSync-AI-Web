Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 25, 2025

SnippetSyncAi ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how our Chrome Extension and website collect, use, and disclose your information.

1. Local-First Philosophy

Our core philosophy is Local-First. By default, your text data belongs to you.

  • Private Snippets: Stored 100% locally on your device using `chrome.storage.local`. We cannot see, read, or access them.
  • Cloud Sync (Optional): If you choose to enable Cloud Sync, your snippets are stored in Firebase Firestore with your user ID. Only you can access your private snippets.

2. Firebase Services

We use Google Firebase to provide authentication and database services:

  • Firebase Authentication: Manages user login via email/password or Google Sign-In. Firebase stores your email address and authentication tokens.
  • Firebase Firestore: Stores public community snippets and your synced private snippets (if you enable sync). Data is stored in Google Cloud servers.
  • Firebase Privacy: Review Firebase's privacy policy at firebase.google.com/support/privacy

3. Community & Public Data

Public Snippets: If you explicitly choose to "Publish" a snippet to the Community Hub:

  • That snippet becomes publicly accessible to all logged-in users.
  • We store your display name (from your account) as the author.
  • You grant us a license to display, distribute, and allow others to install these snippets.
  • Warning: Do not publish private keys, passwords, or personal data in public snippets.

4. Data Collection

We collect minimal data:

  • Account Info: Email address and display name (via Firebase Auth).
  • Usage Stats: Anonymous aggregate data (e.g., "Total public snippets") to improve the service.
  • No Keystroke Logging: We do not track your typing or browsing history.

5. Data Security

Your data is protected by Firebase's industry-standard security measures, including encryption in transit (HTTPS) and at rest. You control your data and can delete your account at any time.

6. Your Rights

  • You can delete your account and all associated data at any time.
  • You can export your snippets from the extension.
  • You can unpublish community snippets you've shared.

7. Contact

If you have questions, contact us at hello@snippetsyncai.app
