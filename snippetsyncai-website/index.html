FAQ

Is my data safe if I use Sync?

Yes. When you use Cloud Sync, your snippets are encrypted. If you choose not to login, your data never leaves your Local Storage.

Do I need an account?

No! You can use SnippetSyncAi completely as a guest for local snippets. Account creation is only needed for Sync and Community features.

Where does it work?

It works on any website! We support Google Chrome, Firefox, Microsoft Edge, Brave, and most modern browsers.
